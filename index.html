Our 4-Step Process

How we ensure a perfect finish every time.

01

Site Assessment

We inspect your subfloor for moisture, levelness, and structural integrity, taking precise laser measurements.

02

Surface Prep

The most crucial step. We apply self-leveling compounds, acoustic underlays, or moisture barriers as required.

03

Installation

Our certified technicians lay the material maintaining necessary expansion gaps and perfect pattern alignment.

04

Final Handover

We clean the site, install matching skirting and transition profiles, and perform a joint quality inspection.
